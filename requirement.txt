## 2. VISUAL DESIGN SYSTEM (UI FOUNDATION)

### 2.1 Dark UI Strategy (Research-Backed)

Dark mode is ideal for:

* Long study sessions
* Video consumption
* Focused environments

#### Background Colors

* Primary: `#0B0F1A` (deep space blue)
* Secondary panels: `#111827`
* Cards: `rgba(255,255,255,0.03)`

‚ö†Ô∏è Never pure black ‚Üí causes eye strain

---

### 2.2 3D Depth Without Heavy Graphics

We simulate 3D using:

* Layered cards
* Soft shadows
* Blur + transparency (glassmorphism)

#### Card Style

* Rounded: 16‚Äì24px
* Shadow:

  ```
  0 10px 30px rgba(0,0,0,0.4)
  ```
* Subtle hover lift (translateY)

This creates **perceived depth**, not heavy 3D rendering ‚Üí keeps performance high.

---

### 2.3 Accent Colors (Psychology-Driven)

Use **1 primary neon + 1 secondary** only.

Example:

* Primary: Neon Cyan `#22D3EE`
* Secondary: Electric Purple `#A855F7`

Usage:

* CTA buttons
* Active states
* Progress bars
* AI features

Never overuse ‚Üí accent = attention.

## 3. TYPOGRAPHY SYSTEM (READABILITY FIRST)

### Font Pairing

* Headings: **Inter / Space Grotesk**
* Body: **Inter**

Why:

* Neutral
* High readability
* Modern SaaS feel

### Hierarchy

* H1: 28‚Äì32px (mobile), 40px (desktop)

* Body: 14‚Äì16px

## 4. MOTION & ANIMATION (NOT DECORATION)

### Principle

> Animation must **explain**, not entertain.

### Where Motion Is Used

* Page transitions (fade + slide)
* Card hover elevation
* Button press feedback
* Loading skeletons
* Progress updates

### Motion Rules

* Duration: 150‚Äì300ms
* Easing: ease-out
* Never animate layout shifts aggressively

Result:

* Interface feels alive
* No distraction during study
